Short Biography: Ya Zhang is currently a Professor in Cooperative Medianet Innovation Center, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She holds a Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University and a PhD degree in Information Sciences and Technology from Pennsylvania State University. Her research interest is mainly on machine learning with applications to multimedia and healthcare. Before joining Shanghai Jiao Tong University, she was a tenure-track assistant professor at the University of Kansas, and later, a Senior R&D Manager at Yahoo! Labs where she led a research team to enhance the search quality of Yahoo's international markets. Dr. Zhang is currently the Vice Dean of the Digital Medical Research Institute at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, the Deputy Director of the Intelligent Healthcare Research Center at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, and the Chief AI Scientist at the State Key Laboratory of Ultra-high-definition Video and Audio Production and Presentation. She has played pivotal roles in national-level and provincial projects in AI and smart healthcare, including being the Chief Expert for a project under the Ministry of Science and Technology's 863 Program and leading a major project under the Science and Technology Innovation 2030 initiative. Dr. Zhang has published over 200 scholarly articles in high-impact international journals and conferences, with her work being cited over 10,000 times according to Google Scholar. She was selected as a Leading Talent under the National High-Level Talent Special Support Program, has received several awards for her contributions to technology and science, including the Shanghai Technology Invention First Prize (2022), EURASIP Best Paper Award for the SPEECH COMMUNICATION Journal (2019), and recognized as an Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Advisor by the Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence (2018).
