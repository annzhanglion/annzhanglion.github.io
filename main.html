Short Biography: Ya Zhang is currently a Distinguished Professor in School of Artificial Intelligence, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She holds a Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University and a PhD degree in Information Sciences and Technology from Pennsylvania State University. Her research focuses on machine learning applications in multimedia and healthcare. Dr. Zhang's career has spanned academia and industry, including roles as a tenure-track assistant professor at the University of Kansas and a Senior R&D Manager at Yahoo! Labs, where she led initiatives to improve search quality. Currently, she holds key positions as Vice Dean of the Digital Medical Research Institute, Deputy Director of the Intelligent Healthcare Research Center at the Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, and Chief AI Scientist at the State Key Laboratory of Ultra-high-definition Video and Audio Production and Presentation. She has played pivotal roles in national-level and provincial projects in AI and smart healthcare, including being the Chief Expert for a project under the Ministry of Science and Technology's 863 Program and leading a major project under the Science and Technology Innovation 2030 initiative. Dr. Zhang has published over 200 scholarly articles in high-impact international journals and conferences, with her work being cited over 10,000 times according to Google Scholar. Her achievements are recognized through various awards and honors, including being named a Leading Talent under the National High-Level Talent Special Support Program (2023), receiving the First Prize of the Shanghai Technological Invention Award (2022), and earning the EURASIP Best Paper Award (2019), and recognized as an Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Advisor by the Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence (2018).
